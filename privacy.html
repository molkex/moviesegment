Privacy Policy

Application: Movie Segment Publisher · Last updated: 15 June 2026

Movie Segment Publisher ("the App") is a tool that publishes short video clips to the user's own connected social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube) through those platforms' official APIs, on the user's own instruction. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is used.

What we access

  • Account authorization (OAuth tokens): when you connect an account, the platform gives the App an access token so it can upload videos to your own account. The App never sees or stores your password.
  • Basic profile info: open id and display name of the connected account, used only to confirm which account content is published to.
  • Video files and captions that you choose to publish.

How we use it

Accessed data is used for one purpose only: to upload and publish the video content you select to your own connected accounts, at your direction. We do not use it for any other purpose.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not post anything without your instruction.
  • We do not access content from accounts other than the ones you connect.

Storage and retention

Access tokens are stored securely and used only to perform the uploads you request. You can revoke the App's access at any time from your TikTok, Instagram, or Google account settings; revoked tokens stop working immediately.

Third-party platforms

Publishing happens through the official APIs of TikTok, Meta (Instagram), and Google (YouTube). Their handling of your data is governed by their own privacy policies.
